Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence by machines.
Machine Learning is a subset of Artificial Intelligence that allows systems to learn from data.
Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) is a system that retrieves relevant information before generating a response.
MongoDB is a NoSQL database that stores data in JSON-like documents.
Vector embeddings are numerical representations of text used for similarity search.
Cosine similarity measures how similar two vectors are.
Chunking is the process of splitting large text into smaller pieces for embedding.
Sentence Transformers generate dense vector embeddings for text.
